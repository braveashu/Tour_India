Kolkata

Food

Rosogolla

A traditional Bengali sweet made from chhena and dipped in sugar syrup.

Macher Jhol

A traditional spicy fish curry, commonly prepared in Bengali households.

Places

Victoria Memorial

A large marble building dedicated to Queen Victoria, located in the heart of Kolkata.

Howrah Bridge

An iconic cantilever bridge over the Hooghly River, connecting Kolkata and Howrah.

Monuments

Indian Museum

The largest and oldest museum in India, housing a vast collection of artifacts.

Culture

Cultural Hub

Kolkata is known as the cultural capital of India, with a rich heritage of literature, art, and music.

Festivals

Festivals

Kolkata celebrates festivals such as Durga Puja, Diwali, and Saraswati Puja with great zeal.

History

Historical Significance

Kolkata has a rich history as a major colonial city, playing a crucial role in India's independence movement.
