Delhi

Food

Chole Bhature

A popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpeas served with fried bread.

Paratha

A type of flatbread originating from the Indian subcontinent, stuffed with various fillings.

Places

India Gate

A war memorial located in New Delhi, dedicated to the soldiers of the Indian Army.

Red Fort

A historic fort in the city of Delhi, which served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors.

Monuments

Qutub Minar

A minaret that forms part of the Qutub complex, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi.

Culture

Diverse Culture

Delhi is a melting pot of various cultures, with a rich heritage of art, music, and dance.

Festivals

Festivals

Delhi celebrates a variety of festivals such as Diwali, Holi, and Eid with great zeal.

History

Historical Significance

Delhi has a long and rich history, serving as the capital of various empires over centuries.
