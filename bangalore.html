Bangalore

Food

Masala Dosa

A popular South Indian dish made with fermented rice and lentil batter, filled with spicy potato filling.

Idli Vada

Steamed rice cakes (Idli) and crispy lentil donuts (Vada) served with chutneys and sambar.

Places

Cubbon Park

A historic park in the heart of Bangalore, known for its lush greenery and walking paths.

Lalbagh Botanical Garden

An iconic botanical garden and glasshouse featuring rare plants from around the world.

Monuments

Bangalore Palace

A beautiful palace built in Tudor style architecture with fortified towers, arches, green lawns, and elegant woodcarvings.

Culture

Vibrant Culture

Bangalore is a melting pot of various cultures, with a rich history of classical music, dance, and art.

Festivals

Festivals

Bangalore celebrates a variety of festivals such as Diwali, Dussehra, and the Bangalore Karaga with great fervor.

History

Rich History

Bangalore has a fascinating history dating back to the 16th century, with landmarks and monuments that tell its story.
