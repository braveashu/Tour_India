Explore Indore

Discover the cultural and culinary delights of Indore.

Places to Visit

Rajwada Palace

One of the popular tourist attractions of Indore, Rajwada Palace is one of the oldest structures too. The palace was constructed by Malhar Rao Holkar, the founder of Holkar Dynasty in the year 1747 A.D. The palace was his residence and remained so till the year 1880 A.D.

Pitra Parvat

Pitra Parvat is not just an important religious spot; it has breathtaking panoramas of Indore. The mountain is located on the top, it is a peaceful retreat with lush vegetation.

Kanch Mandir

Kanch Mandir, literally Temple of Glass, is a famous Jain temple in Indore, built by Sir Seth Hukumchand Jain. The construction began sometime around 1903.
